The entities should be
Stores with a composite / tuple primary key of Company and Store number, (unique) address, and a phone number with stretch to add multiple phone numbers to a single store.


Customer will have a GUUID FName, Optional MName, LName, should have optional address and phone number.

The relationship should be 
Store to Customer Many to Many
Employee to Store Many to One
Many items to Many stores, but each store having a different number of items.
